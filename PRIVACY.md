# Privacy Policy – Forger

**1. Information Collected**  
Forger collects only:

- Discord Guild ID (if used in a server)
- Channel ID (if used in a server)
- Username

No other personal data is collected, regardless of whether the Bot is used in a server or on a personal account.

**2. Use of Information**  
Collected data is used solely for:

- Internal analytics to improve the Bot
- Caching to optimize performance

**3. Data Sharing**  
Forger does not share any data with third parties.

**4. Data Storage and Security**

- Data is stored securely and only as long as needed for internal analytics and caching.
- No profiling or analytics are performed for specific servers or individual users beyond improving the Bot.

**5. User Rights**  
Users can request deletion of their data by contacting the Bot developer.

**6. Changes to This Policy**  
The Privacy Policy may be updated. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any changes.
