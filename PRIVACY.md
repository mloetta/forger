# Privacy Policy for Pocket Tool

_Last updated: November 9, 2025_

**1. Introduction**
Pocket Tool is a Discord bot and related web services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

**2. Data Collection**
Pocket Tool may collect the following types of data:

- Discord user IDs, usernames, and profile information (e.g., avatars, badges) for command responses and features.
- Guild (server) IDs and information for server-specific features.
- Voting data from Top.gg (user ID, vote count, last vote time).
- Analytics and diagnostic data (e.g., server count, memory usage, uptime) for bot statistics and health monitoring.

**3. Data Usage**
We use collected data to:

- Provide bot features and respond to user commands.
- Manage server-specific settings and roles.
- Track votes and reward users for Top.gg interactions.
- Monitor and improve the stability and performance of the bot.

**4. Data Storage**

- Data is stored securely using industry-standard practices.
- User data is stored in a database and is only accessible to authorized processes.
- No sensitive personal information (such as passwords or emails) is collected or stored.

**5. Data Sharing**

- We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except as required for integrations (e.g., Top.gg) or by law.
- Some data may be shared with Discord as part of bot operation.

**6. Cookies and Tracking**

- The Pocket Tool website does not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.
- API endpoints may log requests, including IP addresses, for security and diagnostic purposes.

**7. Third-Party Services**

- Pocket Tool integrates with Discord and Top.gg. Please refer to their privacy policies for details on their data handling.

**8. User Rights and Data Retention**

- Users have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of their personal data in accordance with applicable laws.
- Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.
- To request deletion or access to your data, contact the bot owner via Discord or the support server.

**9. Security**

- We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

**10. Children's Privacy**

- Pocket Tool is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

**11. Changes to This Policy**

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here, and continued use of Pocket Tool after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

**12. Contact**

- For questions or requests regarding your data, please contact the bot owner via Discord or the support server.
